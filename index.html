Travel to northern Iran

I want to take you on an exciting journey from Tehran to the Caspian Sea

  1. We start the journey from the very beautiful Haraz road.
  2. After seeing the high mountain of Damavand and passing cities along the way, we reach the city of Amol.
  3. From Amol we go to Babolsar city to have lunch by the Caspian Sea and enjoy the sea.
  4. Then we go to the forest for lunch to hike in the forest for an hour.
  5. Then we go to the coastal town of Khazarshahr to rest in one of the villas of this city.

The trip started at 5 am Iranian time and we drove from Tehran to Babolsar via Haraz Road for about three hours. On the way we saw beautiful natural scenery and had breakfast in one of the restaurants along the way.
After about 9 o'clock we arrived in Babolsar and rested a little on the beds by the Caspian Sea and had lunch at a beach restaurant. After having lunch and some rest, we went to the forests of Babolsar and fought for about 3 hours. Finally, we went to a beach villa in Khazarshahr town and spent the night there.
